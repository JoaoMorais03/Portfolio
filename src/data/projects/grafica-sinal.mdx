## What it is

A complete rewrite of GraficaSinal.pt — an e-commerce site for a print and graphics company. The existing site was outdated and slow, so the client wanted a ground-up rebuild with a modern stack.

## Approach

Building the new storefront with Next.js and React on the frontend, Node.js and Express on the backend. The focus is on performance, cleaner UX, and making it easier to maintain long-term.

## Status

Currently in development. This is a client project through Flavibyte — one of the side projects I've taken on outside of my day job at Prozis.
